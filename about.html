  My new life in Sweden

  Being an exchange student to Linnaeus University was something that I have never imagined. I have alwayas wanted to experience what is like being an international student. The country where I come from, Albania, is completly different from Sweden in all aspects.
  1. Food
    For example, I like Traditional Albanian food such as:
    • Meat dishes
    • Soups
    • Pies
    2. In Sweden you can mostly find:
    • Raggmunk & Lingonberries
    • Meatballs
    • Salmon
    Well there is quite a difference...
  2. People
    In Sweden people are very calm and try to avoid any conflict, meanwhile Albanians are vey hot-blooded and tend to raise their voice for nothing.
  3. Celebrations
    Swedish celebrate:
    • Midsummer
    • Lucia
    • Christmas
    We, Albanians celebrate:
    • Kurban Bajram
    • Fiter Bajram
    • Christmas
